What is Nutritional Therapy?

Nutritional Therapy Practitioners® (NTPs) assess the bio-individual needs of each client and create a personalized, holistic plan for nutrition and lifestyle change. We don't just make a plan- we educate, coach, and encourage you through the process, making adjustments as needed along the way. We specialize in:

Digestive Health
Food Sensitivities
Metabolic Health
Hormone Balance
Detoxification
